Born in Milan - Italy, in 2002, I had a passion for all things technical since I was a kid. Inspired by my grandad's career as a mechanical engineer in the Italian automotive scene, I attended a scientific highschool, strengthening my interest on the subject.
A BSc in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico of Milano, Italy's best technical univeristy, turned out to be the perfect choice for my Undergraduate studies
After graduation, I seeked new experiences in different settings, and chose TUDelft's MSc in Mechanical Engineering.

Outside of the engineering world, my main interests are ice hockey and roadtrips.

I started playing ice hockey in 2023, in Milano, and immediately fell in love for the sport. Not only is it fun to practice, but I also found it to be a great way to connect with new people and build a team.

During summer break, I always squeeze in at least one trip with my friends or my family. I love the feeling of beeing on the road, with nothing but a cool picture as a destination, not knowing what the itinerary or the stops will be.
In my dad's VW bus, I have taken trips across Italy, Europe and North Africa, meeting incredible people and discovering new cultures. Unplugging from the technical side of my life allows me to recharge my creativity, and get back to work with a fresh mind and new ideas.
